Welcome to the website of Kadi Liis Saar

An interdisciplinary scientist and inventor with a background (BA & MEng) in chemical engineering and biotechnology and a PhD in biophysical chemistry.

Currently an EPSRC post-doctoral research fellow at the Centre for Misfolding Diseases, Department of Chemistry, University of Cambridge, UK. And a mum.

My research interests revolve around developing new technologies for healthcare, in particular new methods for studying proteins and their interactions. Read more about my research interests here.
