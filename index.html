Website of Kadi Liis Saar

An interdisciplinary scientist and inventor with a background in chemical engineering and biotechnology (BA & MEng) and biophysical chemistry (PhD).

Currently a Junior Research Fellow at St. John's College, University of Cambridge and a Schmidt Science Fellow working between the Centre for Misfolding Diseases and the Maxwell Centre.

My interests revolve around developing new experimental technologies and computational approaches for healthcare, in particular for the study of proteins and their interactions. My areas of expertise span from micron and nano scale fabrication and flow engineering, single-molecule spectoscopy and single cell studies to advanced data analysis. Read more about my research here.

I am a mum of two kids and possibly the world's youngest mother to have earned a PhD degree?
